Milan

Milan is one of the most important cities of Europe. It is best known for its fashion, art, culture and tourism. It is the first most populous city of Italy. It also hosts the Italian stock exchange. Milan's theatres, museums and landmarks make it a popular tourist destination and has a high number of accredition from the Michelin guide and is home to major football clubs.
